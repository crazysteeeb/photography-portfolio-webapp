Where are you based, and do you travel?

I am based out of Bruner, MO but travel all over the Southwest part of the state. I love to travel so if you have a place in mind I will be there. It does require a travel fee for mileage depending on the location.

When will we get our photos?

Typically you will receive a few sneak peek photos within 48 hours of the shoot. The Full album will be published and sent to you through email within 4-6 weeks depending on what type of session you have purchased.

How will I receive my pictures?

You will receive your album in the email that is given, once you have purchased the session and editing is complete. This will be a digital album that you will be able to download and print.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, the deposit which is typically $25, is due at the time you book the appointment.
